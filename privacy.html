Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 25, 2026

AI Ghost Writer LLC ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Tales 4 Tots mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Story Inputs: When you create a story, you provide a character name, character description, story idea, and preferences such as genre, age range, and story length. This information is sent to our servers to generate your story and is not stored after the story is delivered to your device.

Purchase Information: If you subscribe or purchase add-ons, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment or billing information.

Device Storage: Your saved stories, audio files, and app preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or sync this data to any external server.

Third-Party Services

Tales 4 Tots uses the following third-party services to generate content:

  • Anthropic (Claude API): Your story inputs are sent to Anthropic's API to generate story text. Anthropic's privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.
  • Speechify API: Generated story text is sent to Speechify's API to create audio narration. Speechify's privacy policy is available at speechify.com/privacy.

These services process data only for the purpose of generating your requested content. We do not share your information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Retention

Story inputs are transmitted to third-party services for real-time content generation and are not retained by us after delivery. Saved stories and audio are stored only on your device and can be deleted at any time by removing them from the app or uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

Tales 4 Tots is designed for use by families. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Story inputs are provided by the user operating the device and are not stored beyond the generation session. We do not require account creation, login, or any personally identifiable information to use the app.

Analytics and Tracking

Tales 4 Tots does not use any analytics frameworks, tracking pixels, or advertising SDKs. We do not track your usage or behavior within the app.

Security

All data transmitted between the app and our servers uses HTTPS encryption. We take reasonable measures to protect the information processed through our services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
